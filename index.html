Instructions
Your goal is to kill the Corrupted Hunllef () as fast as possible.
You do this by creating weapons (a bow, a staff, or a halberd) and upgrading them to their perfected versions with a bowstring, an orb, or a spike, respectively.
The bowstring is obtained from the darkbeast (), the orb from the dragon (), and the spike from the bear ().

To create a basic weapon, you need a weapon frame, which you can obtain from a rat ( 50% chance), a scorpion ( 100% chance), or by killing any of the demibosses ().
You then return to the home tile () and create/upgrade the weapon.

From the home tile, you may also create potions (from herbs ), cook fish (), and create armor.

Basic armor is created with 1 ore (), 1 fluff (), and one bark (), and can then be upgraded with the same.

All of this comes together to give you more of a chance at defeating the hunllef!
